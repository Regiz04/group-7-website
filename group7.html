Gender Stereotypes

What is gender stereotypes?

-Gender stereotypes are usually dictated by people to other people that women and men fulfill their responsibilities based on their gender.

-Gender stereotypes affects people mental health because of the gender roles and expectations

Why do we need to stop gender stereotypes?

-Gender stereotypes effects men and women self-esteem

-Gender stereotypes lead to unequal treatment of men and women

-Gender stereotypes can create toxic environment

Our goal

-The goal is to explain, while also maintaining, men's past leadership over women by displaying a clearly rude discriminatory attitude or worldview toward women or certain men, which creates unfriendly speech, bullying, and homophobic behavior.

Our solution

-Our solution is to develop open discussions around gender stereotypes to raise awareness and understanding, especially to people who follow old traditions.

-We chose open discussion because open discussion can create a safe space to know that they are not alone and open discussion can also help people to be more aware about gender stereotypes
